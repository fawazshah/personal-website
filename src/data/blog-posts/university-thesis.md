The topic of my final year dissertation at Imperial College was <i>Detecting Political Bias On Reddit With Transfer Learning.</i> I researched large language models to predict the political bias of news articles based on the related comments on social media websites, and vice versa, focusing on Reddit due to its unique subreddits feature.

You can read the project report [here](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/fawazshah/MEng-Project-Report/main/Project%20Final%20Report.pdf).
